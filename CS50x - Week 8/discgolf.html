Discgolf

Why play disc golf? Disc golf is the perfect community sport, it's cheap, and it is easy for anyone to learn and play. Disc golfers get great exercise, and great sense of community develops around a local course. Disc golf can attract young people to use parks and open spaces, and help them develop a feeling of ownership and responsibility for this community space. Disc golf is an ideal activity for community groups and schools, because it is accessible to everyone, fun, and a great learning opportunity for everything from physics to physical fitness.

Disc golf is a game that can easy be embraced by a large and diverse pool of participants. Young players are taking up the game as courses show up in their hoods.

College students are embracing disc golf as a low-cost source of social interaction. Families are not only playing locally, but are discovering great appeal in taking disc golf vacations to beautiful course locations.

Since it takes very little financial commitment and the learning curve is so quick, disc golf has very few limitations to its growth and public appeal. New players are flooding to courses as quickly as they are created.
